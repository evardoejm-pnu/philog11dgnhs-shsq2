Weber’s Rationalization

Max Weber described the "Iron Cage" of modern society—a world driven by efficiency, logic, and cold rules.

  • Efficiency: Systems prioritize speed over individual needs.
  • Calculability: We value what can be measured (grades, likes, sales).
  • Predictability: Every interaction follows a pre-set script.

Personal vs. Transactional

In a Personal relation, you are a unique mystery. In a Transactional system, you are a "User."
