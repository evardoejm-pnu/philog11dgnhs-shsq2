Today's Focus: Demonstrating talents of the marginalized through solidarity.

The Ethics of Representation

How do we showcase others' talents without being patronizing?

  • Pity: Looking down on others (maintains a power imbalance).
  • Solidarity: Walking alongside others (acknowledges equal dignity).

Our job is to amplify voices, not speak over them.

Empowerment & Allyship

An Ally uses their platform to advocate for those who are often silenced.

  • Inclusive Design: Creating a world where everyone can participate.
  • Social Entrepreneurship: Business that solves community problems.

The Character Script

Intersubjectivity is a project. You choose who you become through how you treat others.

When you choose to see talent instead of disability, you are "authoring" a version of yourself that is an ally.

Activity: The Ally's Manifesto

Writing Task (5-7 Sentences):

  1. Choose a marginalized group (e.g., street vendors, children with autism).
  2. Identify one specific talent or strength they have.
  3. Write a plan/pledge on how you will advocate for that talent.
  4. Ensure you focus on their Dignity, not their "need for help."
