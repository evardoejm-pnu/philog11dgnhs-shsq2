The Prudence Blueprint

"Prudence is the ability to see the future in the present moment."

Today's Focus: How to make choices that you won't regret.

The Choice

"You find a leaked copy of the Final Exam. It's your only chance to keep your scholarship."

Discussion: Does having a "good reason" (scholarship) make a "bad action" (cheating) prudent?

What is Prudence?

  • Right Reason Applied to Action.
  • It is the "Charioteer" that steers your life.
  • It turns values into actual behaviors.

The 3 Acts

1. Counsel: Gathering facts & advice.

2. Judgment: Weighing options vs. Values.

3. Command: Executing the decision.
